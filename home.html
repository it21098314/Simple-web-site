Welcome to Unawatuna Srilanka

Unawatuna is a coastal town located in the Southern Province of Sri Lanka. It is situated about 5 kilometers south of Galle and is a popular tourist destination known for its stunning beaches, clear waters, and vibrant coral reefs.

Unawatuna Beach is the main attraction of the town, and it is considered to be one of the best beaches in Sri Lanka. The beach is lined with palm trees, and the water is calm and perfect for swimming and snorkeling. There are many water sports activities available such as jet skiing, surfing, and diving..
